About Us - Sun Gallery, Hayward

Located in central Hayward, the Sun Gallery is a community-based art institution with a mission of providing community interest in the arts. Since 1976, the Sun Gallery has stimulated that interest and expanded the image of the City of Hayward as a center that reflects the city's vast and incredible diversity.

The gallery acts in partnership with local institutions of education and government, buisnesses and corporations, and community service organizations to develop quality visual arts programming that welcomes and engage audiences of all ages, in order to enhance the relationship between the visual arts and day-to-day life experiences.
