Education at the Sun Gallery

The Gallery offers a variety of classes for all ages and purposes, from family Saturday classes to field trips to even art parties. Our instructors are professionals form a variety of disciplines, such as airbrushing, children's art, oil painting, and even more. Classes range from introductory techniques to more advanced practices, suitable for many ages and skill levels. Call us at 510-581-4050 to register.
